Our Story

Artistry Beyond
The Ordinary

Light The Way Tattoo (LTW) is a personal, custom tattoo studio located on the outskirts of the 417 area — just far enough off the beaten path to feel like a hidden gem, and close enough to be your go-to studio in Springfield, MO.

Lead artist Daniel runs a by-appointment-only studio, ensuring every client receives his full attention, creativity, and care. From your first consultation to the final needle stroke, the experience is stress-free, personal, and unforgettable.

Whether you're a first-timer with nerves or a seasoned collector with a vision, Daniel's warmth, humor, and skill will make you feel right at home — and walk out with a masterpiece.
